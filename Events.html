Events and Volunteering

International Pet Month

April 1st

Join us for a series of talks on the benefits of adopting pets and how to get involved with us! You will be able to hear from our team members, vets that collaborate with us and new adoptive pet parents!

Cat Convention

April 5th

London's biggest cat show! This is an amazing opportunity for us to raise funds to support cats with shelters. We are currently looking for volunteers to help us raise as much as possible! Please get in contact if you'd like to get involved!

Vaccination clinic

April 14th

Bring your kitten along to get their vaccinations! We believe all cats should have access to medical support - so come along on the day and make the most of our free service for adopted kitten! All cats can get their vaccination for a small fee.

Kitten Yoga

April 26th

Join us for a yoga class with our adorable & adoptable kittens! The class will be an hour long, all levels welcome. First come first served. What better way to meet your future pet than on the yoga mat?
