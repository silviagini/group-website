Welcome to Save Meow!

Cute and cuddly, small and squishy,
Chubby and old or smelling a little fishy,
Slow and dopey or sharp as a knife,
All our furry friends deserve a second chance at life.
Sometimes their noses can be a little runny,
but more often than not, they're hilariously funny.
4 legs, 3 legs, 2 legs, more,
Their quality of life doesn't have to be poor.
Deaf or blind, mischievous or play-fighty
tiny little munchkins can prove to be mighty.
Some sad backstories but their hearts are forgiving,
giving them a home makes our lives worth living!
There are so many more reasons than all those above,
to open your doors and give them your love.
